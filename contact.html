Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours on business days. For urgent technical issues, we prioritize responses and aim to reply within 4-6 hours during our support hours (9 AM - 6 PM UTC).

Do you offer phone support?

Currently, we provide support exclusively via email and our contact form. This allows us to maintain detailed records of all inquiries and provide thorough, documented responses. However, we're exploring adding live chat support in the near future.

Can I request a custom wishing generator?

Yes! We love creating custom generators for specific occasions or themes. Please use the "Feature Request" category in our contact form and provide as much detail as possible about your needs. We'll evaluate the feasibility and get back to you with options.

Are my generated wishes stored on your servers?

Most of our generators work entirely in your browser for privacy. The wishes you create are typically not stored on our servers unless you explicitly choose to save or share them. For specific details, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

Do you provide technical support for integrations?

We provide limited support for integrating our tools into other platforms. For basic integration questions, we're happy to help. For complex integrations or commercial use, we offer consultation services. Please contact us with your specific requirements.
